================================================================================
WHAT IT DOES
================================================================================

Automatically configures stages for Vision Digital Twin work:

  00_set_units_mm              → Sets metersPerUnit to 0.001 (millimeters)
  10_enable_extensions         → Enables required Omniverse extensions
  20_configure_telecentric     → Adds optical parameters to cameras
  30_normalize_transforms      → Removes scaling, ensures 1:1 dimensions
  40_add_custom_attributes     → Adds Vision DT metadata and controls
  50_check_asset_consistency   → Validates naming and standards

Executes automatically on every stage open in numeric order.
